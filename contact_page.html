Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can you provide a shipping quote? +
We typically provide quotes within 2-4 hours during business hours. For urgent requests, we can provide quotes within 30 minutes by calling our hotline.
Do you offer shipment tracking? +
Yes! All our shipments come with real-time GPS tracking available through our Data Portal. You'll receive tracking information immediately after pickup.
What types of cargo do you handle? +
We handle general freight, temperature-controlled goods, hazardous materials (with proper certification), oversized cargo, and specialized equipment. Contact us for specific requirements.
Do you provide insurance coverage? +
Yes, we offer comprehensive cargo insurance options. Basic coverage is included, and additional coverage can be purchased based on your shipment value and requirements.
What are your payment terms? +
We offer flexible payment terms including Net 30 for established customers, credit card payments, and prepaid options. Corporate accounts are available with credit approval.
