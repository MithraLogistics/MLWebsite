About Mithra Logistics

Pioneering excellence in global logistics solutions since 2010

Our Story

Founded in 2010, Mithra Logistics has grown from a small regional carrier to a globally recognized logistics powerhouse. Our journey began with a simple mission: to provide reliable, efficient, and cost-effective logistics solutions that help businesses thrive in an interconnected world.

What started as a family-owned trucking company has evolved into a comprehensive logistics network spanning 25 countries and serving over 500 satisfied clients. Our commitment to innovation, customer service, and operational excellence has been the driving force behind our remarkable growth.

Our Mission

To deliver world-class logistics solutions that enable our clients to focus on their core business while we handle the complexities of global supply chain management with precision, reliability, and innovation.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted and innovative logistics partner globally, setting new standards for efficiency, sustainability, and customer satisfaction in the transportation and supply chain industry.
